2 lbs pork belly

Instructions

1.Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic

3. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes
4. Add water, whole pepper corn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil.
5. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
6.Put-in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
7. Add salt to taste
8. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
